Welcome to my website!


Hello, thanks for visiting my site! My name is Zailyn Tamayo. I currently live in Southern California with my dog Logan. I am the Residential Networking / User Support Specialist for Scripps College. Last year I received my Master of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering. I am actively looking for a job with greater responsibilities and that more closely aligns with my skills and interests. I love technology and enjoy working in higher education. My current focus is to work on front end web development and to practice more Python. Also, I am studying for RHCSA certification and practicing systems administration. Please check out my Projects page to see what I am working on.

Some of my interests include: music, video games, reading, learning and anime. I love music and listen to it all the time. Video games and watching anime are my main forms of entertainment; I enjoy the accomplishment of completing a game and also enjoy good storylines and plots. I consider myself to be a life-long learner and I am always seeking opportunities to learn new things. In my free time I listen to music and practice coding, or I watch lectures and/or take online courses. I also like to visit museums and libraries whenever I can; I strongly believe in supporting the arts, culture and education. In the near future I plan to add a blog to this site so that I can write more about my interests and about the things I'm working on.

Thank you for taking the time to read through my site. Please feel free to look around and check out my social media links.
